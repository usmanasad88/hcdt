Method 1
Move Assembly Box so that Cylinder Plate is facing upwards
Insert the first ball seat into the cylinder base
Insert the ball into the cylinder base
Insert the second ball seat into the cylinder base
Insert the first cylinder bracket onto the cylinder base
Insert the second cylinder bracket onto the cylinder base
Screw the cylinder cap to the cylinder base
Place the cylinder sub-assembly onto assembly plate 1, aligning one of the cylinder bracket holes to the assembly plate holes
Screw first M8 screw into cylinder bracket hole to fasten cylinder bracket to assembly plate
Screw second M8 screw into cylinder bracket hole to fasten cylinder bracket to assembly plate
Slide the cylinder brackets to the ends of the cylinder
Screw third M8 screw into cylinder bracket hole to fasten cylinder bracket to assembly plate
Screw fourth M8 screw into cylinder bracket hole to fasten cylinder bracket to assembly plate
