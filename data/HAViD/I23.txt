Method 3
Move Assembly Box so that Cylinder Plate is facing upwards
Insert the first ball seat into the cylinder base 
Insert the ball into the cylinder base
Insert the second ball seat into the cylinder base
Insert the first housing onto the cylinder base
Insert the second housing onto the cylinder base
Screw the cylinder cap to the cylinder base
Slide the housings to the ends of the cylinder
Place the cylinder sub-assembly onto assembly plate, aligning one of the housing holes to the assembly plate holes
Screw the first M8 screw into housing hole to fasten housing to assembly plate
Screw the second M8 screw into housing hole to fasten housing to assembly plate
Screw the third M8 screw into housing hole to fasten housing to assembly plate
Screw the fourth M8 screw into housing hole to fasten housing to assembly plate
